Description

In Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship program, students can acquire the skills needed to initiate a small business or enhance an existing one. The curriculum typically covers accounting, marketing, finance, and strategies for fostering economic growth in their chosen business field. Students also gain proficiency in crafting business plans, honing business communication, and mastering management and administration skills. The emphasis is on e mpowering students to act on their ideas and become adept decision-makers in the business realm. Internships are commonly mandatory, offering real-world experience. Admission to a bachelor program in entrepreneurship typically requires a high school diploma or equivalent. A Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship equips individuals with the essential skills to recognize and analyze market and economic opportunities, enabling them to capitalize on these prospects. The primary goal of the course is to cultivate efficient and socially responsible Filipino entrepreneurs.

Course Offer

The Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship (BS Entrep) program of Quezon City University adopts an outcomes-based curriculum as prescribed by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) and in accordance with CMO No. 18, series of 2017. This program provides classroom training and experiential learning. It will help aspiring entrepreneurs acquire skills, values and attitudes that will increase their chances of success.

Career and Skills

What Jobs Can You Pursue With an Entrepreneurship Degree?
Recruiters, Marketers, Sales development representatives, Financial Analyst, Sales Managers, Business Consultants, Financial Advisers, Business analysts, Management consultant, and Chief Executive Officer.

The exploitation of entrepreneurial opportunities may include:
Acquiring financial and material resources, Being responsible for both the venture's success or failure, Risk aversion, Entrepreneurial Skills, Business Management Skills, Communication and Listening Critical and Creative Thinking Skills, Strategic Thinking and Planning Skills, Teamwork and Leadership Skills, and Time Management and Organizational Skills.
